Vulnerable Git tag/branch
Risk - What might be lost by the owner (confidentiality/availability/integrity) or gained by the attacker
Threat Model - What can the adversary (attacker) do?
Vulnerability - What is the software defect that enables the attack?
Exploit - Instructions/script to expose the vulnerability
Patch/Mitigation - How to do we remove the vulnerability?
Media link - (optional) Is there some news worthy compromise that used a vulnerability similar to this?
